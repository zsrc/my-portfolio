About

A bit about me

I'm Zehra, a product designer with a passion for creating experiences that are both beautiful and genuinely useful. I believe good design is invisible — it just works.

I've worked with early-stage startups and established companies across fintech, health, and e-commerce. My process is collaborative and research-driven — I spend as much time talking to users as I do in Figma.

When I'm not designing, I'm usually exploring typography, reading about cognitive psychology, or hunting for the best coffee in town.
